MOLD MITIGATION

Damage from water, if left unattended for any time, may lead to mold growth. People may experience health effects associated with exposure to mold in damp buildings, according to a two-year study by the Institute of Medicine. These health effects may include a runny nose, sneezing, coughing, congestion, aggravation of asthma, sore throat, or inflammation of the sinus.

Biohazard & Trauma Cleanup

"When tragedy hits a home or business, the aftermath is more than a mess — it’s a serious health risk."

Biohazard cleanup requires precision, protection, and people who know exactly what they’re doing. We handle situations most companies can’t and won’t touch. We’re trained, certified, and equipped to decontaminate and restore spaces affected by trauma, death, hazardous waste, and infectious materials. When the scene is overwhelming, we take control — safely, respectfully, and fast.

WHAT WE CLEAN & RESTORE

Trauma & Crime Scene

Bloodborne pathogens, bodily fluids, and contaminated materials. We sanitize, disinfect, and restore the scene to a safe, livable condition while maintaining strict confidentiality.

Unattended Death

We remove biological contaminants, handle odor neutralization, and restore the property with sensitivity for the family involved.

Hoarding Cleanup

We clear, sanitize, and restore severe hoarding environments, removing hazardous materials and making the space safe again.

Biohazard Waste Removal

Sharps, medical waste, infectious materials — handled and disposed of to federal, state, and local standards.

Vehicle Bio Cleanup

Blood, bodily fluids, drug contamination, waste, and trauma incidents inside personal, commercial, or fleet vehicles.

Odor Removal

Advanced deodorizers, ozone treatments, and antimicrobial solutions to fully eliminate odors at the source — not cover them up.
